Privacy Policy

Radius

Radius does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

Radius has no telemetry, analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting service, account system, or network calls.

Local Data

Radius stores local preferences on your device, such as enabled state, corner radius, and startup preference. These settings remain on your device and are used only to keep the app working as configured.

Screen And Input

Radius does not capture your screen, inspect other apps, read window contents, log keyboard input, or log mouse input. Its corner overlays are visual and click-through.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/tatsumioga1/radius.
